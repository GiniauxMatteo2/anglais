Prototype vision

This prototype shows how a hospital can responsibly collect and share data related to environment, genetics and diet — to estimate risks and propose personalized preventive actions.

Simulated features:
  • Structured form for environment, genetics and diet.
  • Simple “estimated risk” calculated from factor weights.
  • Patient dashboard + general recommendations.
